## Task 4 – Email Etiquette and Social Media Guidelines

In this task, I learned about professional email communication and responsible social media usage.
I wrote two professional emails, one requesting an assignment deadline extension and another expressing interest in a summer internship opportunity.
While writing these emails, I learned the importance of using a clear subject line, polite language, proper greetings, and professional sign-offs.

I also created a social media do’s and don’ts checklist to promote responsible online behavior among students.
This task helped me understand that digital communication should always be respectful, clear, and professional because online communication creates a digital impression.
Poor digital communication can create misunderstandings and may affect academic or professional opportunities.
