Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students

Do’s:

1. Keep your profile professional.
2. Use respectful language online.
3. Share academic achievements and projects.
4. Verify information before sharing.
5. Maintain privacy settings on your accounts.

Don’ts:

1. Do not share personal information publicly.
2. Do not post offensive or inappropriate content.
3. Do not spread false information.
4. Do not use abusive language online.
5. Do not share your passwords with anyone.
