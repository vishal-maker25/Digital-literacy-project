Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

1. Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone.
2. Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails.
3. Always check the website URL before entering login details.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
5. Enable two-factor authentication where possible.
6. Do not share personal information on social media.
7. For UPI payments, always verify the receiver before sending money.
8. Do not scan unknown QR codes for payments.
9. Report cybercrime on cybercrime.gov.in.
10. Call helpline number 1930 in case of online financial fraud.
