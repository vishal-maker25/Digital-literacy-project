## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study

In this task, I studied a cybercrime case related to phishing attacks and online fraud. I learned how attackers trick people into sharing personal information such as passwords, OTPs, and bank details by sending fake emails or messages that look genuine. Many people become victims of phishing because they are not aware of such online scams.

While researching this topic, I was surprised to learn how easily people can lose their personal data and money due to small mistakes like clicking unknown links or sharing OTPs. This task helped me understand the importance of online safety and cyber awareness. One habit I will personally change is that I will always verify links and websites before entering any personal information online and I will never share OTP or passwords with anyone.
